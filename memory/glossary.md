# Glossary

## Bull case

The strongest reasonable argument for why an investment might perform well.

## Bear case

The strongest reasonable argument for why an investment might fail or underperform.

## VWCE alternative test

A discipline check asking whether a single stock is truly better than adding to the broad ETF core.

## Thesis drift

When the original reason for owning an investment changes, but the position remains because of emotion or inertia.
