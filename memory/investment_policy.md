# Investment Policy

Vela is built for an ETF-first investor. The default action is to keep broad ETF exposure unless a single-stock thesis clearly passes a higher standard.

## Core principles

- Default benchmark: VWCE or a similarly broad global equity ETF.
- Do not buy a stock just because it is exciting, popular, or recently up.
- Every idea must be compared with simply adding to the ETF core.
- Every idea must include a bear case and a downside scenario.
- Every idea must define what would change the thesis.
- No leverage.
- No options.
- No live trading automation.

## Position sizing guardrails

- Core broad ETFs may be the majority of the portfolio.
- Any single stock should normally stay below 5% of the portfolio.
- Speculative or IPO-like ideas should normally stay below 2%.
- A failed VWCE alternative test means the default label is `ETF-only is better`.
