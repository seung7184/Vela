# Friday — Weekly Portfolio Review

Read:

- `portfolio.csv`
- `watchlist.csv`
- `memory/thesis_log.md`
- all reports from the current week

Create `reports/weekly/YYYY-MM-DD_weekly_review.md`.

Include:

1. What changed this week
2. Portfolio risk summary
3. ETF core assessment
4. Individual stock ideas worth studying
5. Ideas rejected and why
6. Emotional decisions to avoid
7. Next week's study plan
8. One investing concept to learn

Educational use only. Not financial advice.
